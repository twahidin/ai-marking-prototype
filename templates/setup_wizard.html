The app is currently using a temporary SQLite database. This means all your data (classes, assignments, marking results) will be lost when the server restarts or redeploys.
To add a permanent PostgreSQL database on Railway:
1
Go to your Railway project dashboard
2
Click + New in the top right corner
3
Select Database then Add PostgreSQL
4
Click on the new Postgres service, go to Variables, and copy DATABASE_URL
5
Go to your app service's Variables tab, click + New Variable, paste as DATABASE_URL
6
The app will redeploy automatically -- come back to this wizard after
You can continue without a database for now, but we strongly recommend adding one before entering real data.
